The Meet at Capri

April, 2023
El Paso's cocktail & coffee bar, Capri, was filled with excitement as photographers and models gathered for an event that promised to offer a wealth of opportunities. The event, organized by Victoria Acosta and Vivian Serrano, aimed to bring together photographers and models in a casual yet creative setting, and the results were nothing short of stunning.

As the sun set over the Franklins, Capri's ambiance set the stage for an evening of creativity and collaboration. The lounge's chic interiors, with their dimly lit corners and jarrito portraits, provided the perfect backdrop for the photographers to capture the beauty of the models and their surroundings.

The event brought together a diverse group of photographers, ranging from seasoned professionals to budding amateurs, each armed with their unique style and creative vision. The models, hailing from different walks of life, exuded confidence and charisma as they struck poses and interacted with the photographers.

One of the highlights of the evening was the opportunity for the photographers to experiment with different lighting techniques and compositions to capture the perfect shot. Some photographers utilized natural light to create soft and romantic portraits, while others played with dramatic shadows and moody hues to create edgy and avant-garde images.

The photographers exchanged tips and tricks, shared equipment, and even collaborated on impromptu shoots, creating a supportive and inspiring environment that fueled their creativity. The models, too, bonded over their shared passion for modeling, and many of them walked away with new connections and potential opportunities for future collaborations.

However, it wasn't just about the photography and modeling. Capri's delectable cocktails and exceptional DJs also added to the overall experience. The mix of shutter clicks and house music filled the air as the participants mingled, sipped on their favorite cocktails, and indulged in the delicious offerings of the lounge.

As the evening drew to a close, the participants left Capri with memory cards full of stunning images, new friendships, and a renewed sense of inspiration. The meetup event at Capri was an ode to the art of photography and modeling, showcasing the power of collaboration, creativity, and community. Here's to many more such events that continue to inspire and uplift the local photography community in El Paso and beyond.

A heartfelt thank you to the talented photographers and models who shared their breathtaking shots with us. Special thanks to Steven Ramirez, Mario Arzipe, DLA Studios, Lesley, and Kenneth “Stew” Steward for their exceptional photography skills, and to Angel Lu, Victoria Acosta, Judah Arrington, Sofia Terrazas, Kelly Garcia Moreno, Olivia Davis, AZ Arrieta, and Amandalay Bay for their captivating modeling. Additionally, we would like to express our special thanks to Capri, DJ Toxika, RobSau Music, Victoria Acosta, and Vivian Serrano for making these stunning visuals possible.
