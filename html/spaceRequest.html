The Mission

Casa de Cuadros was designed to showcase El Paso talent and make it easier for customers to reach such talent through a centralized platform. More importantly, Casa de Cuadros vows to keep an open conversation with you, the photographic community, in order to improve this platform.

Oboarding

Casa de Cuadros is a wall of photographer profiles. No money transactions or bookings are carried out on the site.

There are two sections for each profile: the images and the contact information. You can see a complete example layout here.

Contact
